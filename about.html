What can I say?

Born in Minnesota, lived three different places in Illinois, 5 years in Detroit, high school in South Korea, University in Wyoming and New Zealand, Colorado and California for a bit, now back in Detroit. I'd like to say it's full circle, but there's a little extra bit hanging off there so it's really more of a balloon.

I've been walking only slightly longer than I could read, writing for only mildly shorter than I could walk. If you blended me up and analyzed my base components, it would be a little bit of everything and a whole lot of words. And I toss 'em 'bout passing fair.


I have experience in:

  • Copywriting
  • Advertising Strategy
  • SEO
  • Front-End Web Development
  • Experimental Research Design


Writing is so much more than the stringing together of pretty words. It requires vision mixed with a vivid imagination. A knack for brevity. Thorough appreciation of the eloquence and grace that drive our language to the heights of its beauty.

And a feel for when a good visual can do all the work.
